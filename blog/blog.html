If you’re curious about my professional background, you can skim my Resume.

I'm 23 years old and live in Atlanta, Georgia. I am currently pursuing my Master’s in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech, where my research focuses on power electronics. I design, model, and prototype converters that integrate distributed energy resources to power a next-gen grid.

In my free time, I enjoy writing, traveling, and working on side projects. I also run tabletop roleplaying games for friends, a new hobby for me! I’m always eager to learn new things and push myself with fresh challenges.

This Journal is both a record and an experiment. Through it, I hope to capture snapshots of thoughts and experiences as I move forward in my academic, professional, and personal journey. To traverse this, you can scroll to view entries in reverse chronological order, or sort by category.

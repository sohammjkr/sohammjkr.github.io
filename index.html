Current Professional Focus

I am an MSECE student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, working on my thesis under Prof. Deepak Divan at the Center for Distributed Energy (CDE) as a Graduate Research Assistant. My research focuses on designing, modeling, and prototyping power converters that integrate distributed energy resources to support the next-generation grid.

I also serve as Chair of the IEEE Power & Energy Society at Georgia Tech, where I lead student initiatives and foster industry engagement. I am passionate about developing practical solutions that accelerate the energy transition and empower communities around the world.
